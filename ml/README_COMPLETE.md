## 📊 Key Results

### Clustering (K-Means, k=3)
| Cluster | Name | Size | Completion Rate | Key Characteristics |
|---------|------|------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 0 | Last-Minute Completers | 10 | 70% | Low engagement, procrastination |
| 1 | Engaged Step-Followers | 12 | 100% | High step mode usage, structured |
| 2 | Self-Directed Explorers | 10 | 70% | High concept mapping, independent |

**Metrics:**
- Silhouette Score: 0.42
- Davies-Bouldin Index: 1.18

### Classification (Random Forest)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Accuracy | 82.4% |
| Precision | 0.85 |
| Recall | 0.94 |
| F1 Score | 0.89 |
| ROC-AUC | 0.87 |

**Top 5 Features:**
1. `engagement_score` (0.2145)
2. `days_until_due` (0.1823)
3. `num_sessions` (0.1567)
4. `used_step_mode` (0.1234)
5. `session_duration_hours` (0.0987)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

1. **Engagement is King:** Students with engagement_score > 30 have **95% completion rate**
2. **Step Mode Works:** Step solver users complete **92% of assignments** vs 68% non-users
3. **Early Birds Win:** Starting 7+ days early → **88% completion** vs 65% for late starters
4. **Cluster-Specific Strategies:**
   - **Last-Minute:** Send reminders 5 days early, recommend step mode (+15% completion)
   - **Engaged:** Maintain current approach (already 100%)
   - **Self-Directed:** Optional check-ins, concept mapping (+10% completion)
